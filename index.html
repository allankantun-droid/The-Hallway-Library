About A.K.

Welcome to The Hallway Library.


My name is Allan Kantun, though most stories here are signed simply as A.K.


I write about forgotten places, strange corridors, memories that refuse to fade, and the quiet spaces between reality and dreams.


This library is a collection of stories, poems and fragments gathered along the way.
